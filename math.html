About Mathematics Department

The Mathematics Department at TopperwalaGyan focuses on fostering innovative research while providing top-notch education. Our faculty members are experts in various fields of mathematics, engaging in research that spans theoretical mathematics to applied sciences.

Faculty Members

Dr. A. Patel (HOD) - Full Professor
Dr. Patel specializes in Algebraic Geometry and has published extensively in international journals.
Dr. B. Singh - Associate Professor
Dr. Singh focuses on Number Theory and has authored several widely-used textbooks in the field.
Dr. C. Verma - Assistant Professor
Dr. Verma is known for his research in Statistical Mathematics and has been awarded for his innovative teaching methods.
Dr. D. Roy - Reader
Dr. Roy specializes in Topology and has been involved in various interdisciplinary research projects.
Dr. E. Kumar - Guest Professor
Dr. Kumar brings industry experience in Computational Mathematics and is currently working on real-world applications of mathematical theories.
