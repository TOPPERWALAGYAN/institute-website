About Chemistry Department

The Chemistry Department at TopperwalaGyan is committed to advancing the field through innovative research and comprehensive education. Our faculty are engaged in pioneering research and are dedicated to providing students with a solid foundation in chemistry.

Faculty Members

Dr. F. Mehta (HOD) - Full Professor
Dr. Mehta specializes in Organic Chemistry and has published over 40 research papers.
Dr. G. Iyer - Associate Professor
Dr. Iyer focuses on Inorganic Chemistry and has authored several influential textbooks.
Dr. H. Yadav - Assistant Professor
Dr. Yadav is known for her research in Biochemistry and has received multiple awards for her contributions.
Dr. I. Chatterjee - Reader
Dr. Chatterjee specializes in Physical Chemistry and has been involved in various collaborative projects.
Dr. J. Kapoor - Guest Professor
Dr. Kapoor brings a wealth of experience from industry and focuses on Chemical Engineering applications.
