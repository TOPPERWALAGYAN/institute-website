About Astrophysics Engineering Department

The Astrophysics Engineering Department at TopperwalaGyan is committed to exploring the universe through innovative research and education. Our faculty members are dedicated to advancing the field of astrophysics while providing high-quality education.

Faculty Members

Dr. Z. Kapoor (HOD) - Full Professor
Dr. Kapoor specializes in Cosmology and has published extensively on dark matter and energy.
Dr. AA. Rao - Associate Professor
Dr. Rao focuses on Stellar Astrophysics and has led significant observational studies.
Dr. BB. Singh - Assistant Professor
Dr. Singh is known for her research in Planetary Science and has contributed to various space missions.
Dr. CC. Verma - Reader
Dr. Verma specializes in Exoplanet Studies and has received multiple grants for her research.
Dr. DD. Joshi - Guest Professor
Dr. Joshi has extensive experience in Space Technology and focuses on applications in astrophysics.
